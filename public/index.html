Hello, I'm Eva!

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented first-year computer science student studying the masters degree ‘Computer Science (Cybersecurity)’ at the University of Aberdeen. After university, I want to join the police force in hopes of developing my knowledge in cybercrime and ways to prevent it.

I became interested in cybersecurity after someone close to me was a victim of identity theft, an experience that affected them for years. The lack of protection and methods to find those responsible exposed the threat of the online world. A lack of knowledge of potential threats combined with a failure to implement preventative measures could result in a very real nightmare. While there have been improvements, I am driven to make a safer online world for others.
